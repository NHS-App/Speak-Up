Speak Up

How It All Works

The Speak Up App is a feedback and user friendly app. It helps us to understand user interests and opinions. Continue to read below to find out information about the app features.

Initiatives

The Intiatives section of the app gives you free time to explore [the event] floor and visit different stalls. Each stall will be listed in the app along with a rating section and a button that allows you find out more bout the initative.

Speakers

At the [enter event name here], a range of speakers will be presenting different topics. To make your experience more informative, the Speakers section of Speak Up gives you a space to ask questions. Towards the end of the speaker's presentaion, questions that were asked by audience members will be displayed on the screen and answered by the speaker.

Pledges

Once you have visisted every Intiative stall and have heard the speakers, the Pledge page will ask you to enter a pledge title and then a desciption. Making a pledge is a way of reminding you of any commitments or promises you've made. Once you have made a pledge it will be automatically saved into our database and through the database we can contact you via email.

Before you are able to experience the features, we will need you to register. This helps us keep in contact with you. Enjoy your experience.
