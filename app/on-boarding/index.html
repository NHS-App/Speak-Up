Speak Up

How It All Works

The Speak Up App is a user friendly feedback and connection app. Allowing all users to enjoy their experience while at [Event name]. Continue to read below to find out information about the app features.

Initiatives

The Initiatives section of the app gives you free time to explore [the event] floor and visit different stalls. Each stall will be listed in the app along with a rating section and a button that allows you find out more bout the initiative.

Speakers

At the [enter event name here], a range of speakers will be presenting different topics. To make your experience more informative, the Speakers section of the app provides you with a space to ask a question. Towards the end of the speaker's presentaion, questions that were asked will appear on the board and then answered by the speaker.

Pledges

Once you have visited every Initiative stall and have heard the speakers, the Pledge page will ask you to enter a pledge title and then a desciption. Making a pledge is a way of reminding you of any commitments or promises you've made. After a certain amount of time, an email with the pledges you've made will be sent.

Before you can see all of these featiures, you will need to register. This is how we will keep in contact with you.
