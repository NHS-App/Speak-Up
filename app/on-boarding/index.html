Speak Up

How It All Works

The Speak Up App is an app that helps it's users voices be heard and also helps you keep in touch with others. Continue to read below to find out information about the app features.

Initiatives

The Intiatives section of the app gives you free time to explore [the event] floor and visit different stalls. Each stall will be listed in the app along with a rating section and a button that allows you find out more bout the initative.

Speakers

At the [enter event name here], a range of speakers will be presenting different topics of their choice. To make your experience more informative, the Speakers section of the app allows you time to ask a speaker a question. Towards the end of the speaker's presentaion, all the questions that were asked by audience members will be put up on the board behind the speaker and answered.

Pledges

Once you have visisted every Intiative stall and have heard the speakers, the Pledge page will ask you to enter a pledge title and then a desciption. Making a pledge is a way of remainding you of any commitments or promises you've made at the event and whatever pledge you've made, The NHS will be able to send you an email with all the pledges you've made.
